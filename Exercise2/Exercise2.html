Mount Kailash

Mount Kailash is a mountain in Ngari prefecture,Tibet Autonomous Region of china. It lies in the kailash Range of the Transhimalaya,in the western part of the Tibetan plateau. Mount Kailash is less than 100km north of the western trijunction of the borders of china,india,and Nepal

There are many reasons one should consider this tour,not the least of which is for the benefits to your health. kailash lies at an altitude of 6,636 meters above sea level,and the hike around the mountain reaches heights of more than 5,300 meters
